# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for people who maintain multilingual knowledge sites, documentation sites, and small publishing systems.

Participants are expected to be respectful, constructive, and patient with contributors of different experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about technical concerns.
- Give feedback on code, docs, and decisions rather than personal traits.
- Keep security issues private until they are responsibly handled.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately derailing issues or pull requests.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private production data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants when needed to keep the project healthy. Security or abuse reports should be sent through GitHub private channels when available.
