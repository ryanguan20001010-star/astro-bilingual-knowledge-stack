# Private Exclusions

This document explains what has been intentionally excluded from this open-source toolkit. These components remain in the original private repository because they are tightly coupled to specific business logic, brand identity, or contain sensitive information.

## Excluded (Kept Private)

1. **Brand Assets & Styling**
   - Specific brand colors, typography, logos, and favicons.
   - Custom SVG illustrations and marketing imagery.

2. **Business Content**
   - Paid reports, courses, and premium content logic.
   - Marketing funnels, lead generation forms, and newsletter integrations (e.g., ConvertKit).
   - Domain-specific calculators and interactive tools.

3. **E-commerce Integrations**
   - Payhip integration logic, product sync scripts, and webhook handlers.

4. **Private Data & Secrets**
   - Real Cloudflare API tokens, Notion database IDs, Google Analytics IDs.
   - Operational analytics data and dashboard generation scripts.
   - Internal business strategy and marketing playbooks.
